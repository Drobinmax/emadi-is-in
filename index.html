His Excellency Ali Shareef Al Emadi is in Washington DC to inaugurate a new hotel

November 7, 2019November 7, 2019 Beverly Ma Uncategorized

Qatar’s Finance Minister His Excellency Ali Shareef Al Emadi led the unveiling of The Conrad Washington DC, the new Qatari Diar venture in Washington, DC. The hotel is also the most recent property in the Conrad Hotels & Resorts portfolio of luxury brands owned by Hilton Worldwide.

Conrad Washington DC is strategically located 14 minutes away from Ronald Reagan International Airport. Union Station is just 9 minutes away from the place. Dulles International Airport is about 40 minutes away. If you’re in Baltimore or Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport, and it’ll take about 47 minutes.

The project has been carried out in a joint venture with Hines Interests Limited Partnership. It also forms part of the global development of the Qatari Group, which has entered more than 24 countries. They designed a 370-room luxury hotel with 62,000 square feet of large retail space in CityCenterDC, a 10-acre, iconic, mixed-use project in the heart of the country’s capital.

According to the CEO of Qatari Diar, the new strategy company focused on speeding the building process of all of its projects under progress. The completion of the venture was complimented by the President of Qatari Diar, who also recognized the efforts of the company’s main partner as well as all the project staff. He also added that the successes of the company were all the result of continued work on the implementation of the plan to achieve the maximum return on real estate investment worldwide.

The Chairman of the Qatari Diar, Sheik Khalid bin Khalifa Al Thani; CEO of Qatari Diar, Eng. Abdullah bin Hamad Al Attiyah; H.E. Sheik Meshaal bin Hamad Al Thani, Qatar’s Ambassador to the United States; Muriel Bowser, Mayor of Washington, and several prominent figures in the United States were all present at the inauguration ceremony.
